## Usage Notes

- **Template A** is best when you have a real user willing to share. Replace details with their actual story.
- **Template B** works as a general recommendation from team/ambassadors.
- **Template C** is designed to spark conversation — works well in active groups.
- **Template D** is for July-August urgency push.
- Always keep it casual and conversational — WeChat groups reject anything that feels like an ad.
- Don't post more than 1x per week in any single group.
- Best timing: evening (8-10pm China time) when students are browsing.
